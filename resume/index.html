My Professional Evolution

Hello! I’m Zaida Carrion Rodriguez, a proud Puerto Rican who has embraced the vibrant life of Florida since 2004. With a Bachelor’s degree in Accounting and over 25 years of experience as a CPA, I’ve navigated the intricate world of finance while currently working remotely for a company in Puerto Rico.

However, my heart has always yearned for creativity. Six years ago, I took a leap of faith into the world of graphic design by enrolling in a Web Design and Development certificate program at Florida State University. This transformative experience ignited a passion within me, inspiring me to explore the depths of design through over 40 online courses on platforms like Udemy, Skillshare, LinkedIn Learning, Bring Your Own Laptop, and Coursera. Each course has been a stepping stone, enriching my understanding and skills in this dynamic field.

Recognizing that a solid foundation in graphic design is essential for impactful web design, I began freelancing as a side project, turning my passion into tangible creations. After years of self-directed learning, I realized that formal education would further elevate my skills. This realization led me to pursue an Associate Degree in Graphic Design at Seminole State College, where I am excited to refine my craft and prepare for a career that seamlessly blends creativity with strategic thinking.

Join me on this journey as I continue to evolve, merging my analytical background with my artistic aspirations to create compelling designs that resonate with audiences.
